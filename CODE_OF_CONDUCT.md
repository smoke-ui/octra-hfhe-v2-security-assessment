# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Contributors should be precise, respectful, evidence-led, and careful with sensitive material. Disagreement about cryptographic claims is expected; personal attacks, harassment, fabricated evidence, credential publication, or unsafe testing are not acceptable.

## Expected behavior

- Critique methods and evidence, not people.
- State uncertainty and correct errors openly.
- Preserve reproducible negative results.
- Respect challenge scope and coordinated disclosure.
- Redact private keys, mnemonics, tokens, and wallet files.
- Credit prior work and upstream sources.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Testing unrelated wallets, users, or infrastructure
- Publishing live credentials or exploit material before responsible notification
- Falsifying outputs, identities, authorship, or successful recovery
- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sustained disruption

## Enforcement

Report conduct or sensitive safety concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may edit or remove unsafe content, close discussions, reject contributions, or restrict participation. Security findings should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
